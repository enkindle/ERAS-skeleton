Get Involved!

Since being founded in 1994 the Edmonton Reptile and Amphibian Society has worked to acheieve it's goals of public education and habitat conservation. The membership is part of what makes ERAS the great society that it is, and without member involvement it is impossible to achieve these goals. Your membership in ERAS gives you the opportunity to contribute to these goals and make a difference for the hobby that you share a passion with many other people.

Volunteering is another way that you can truly make a difference with the society's goals.
